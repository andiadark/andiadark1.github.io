Why I love music?

   For as long as I can remeber I have always loved msuic. Growing up we always had a piano, I would sit next to my mother or grandmother and watch them. Then, as soon as they would get up, I would try to play what I remembered. I will never claim to be the next Beethoven but I am pretty good at copying what I hear, within reason. My grandmother taught me some of moonlight sonata on the piano and I have a goal of learning the whole song eventually.

My Music History

  I started with piano lessons in 1st grade, played violin in 6th/7th grade, and flute lessons starting at the end of 7th grade. In first grade I got my first piano tutor. I remember my first piano concert in 2nd grade in elementary school where I played Somewhere Over the Rainbow and Amazing Grace.

  After graduating middle school, I focused more on playing Flute. When I entered high school I joined the marching band.

12th grade marching band photo

I also joined the Symphonic band where I remember getting the honor of getting to be first chair and playing Variations on a Korean Folk Song. Marching band had its ups and downs but it is always something I will cherish.
