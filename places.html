Tampa Dharma Vegan Kitchen In Armature Works in Tampa, Florida there is a vegan food spot called Dharma Vegan Kitchen. When I visited I ordered the Florida green tomato which is a sandwich that has fried green tomatoes and other vegetables with a side of vegan garlic mac & cheese.
Orlando Orlando Biscuit Company In Orlando, Florida there is a shop called Orlando biscuit company and they sell things like chicken tenders and vegan biscuits. When I had the opportunity to visit, I ordered the three piece tender meal with a biscuit. The food was really enjoyable and it reminded me of the chicken tenders I ate before going vegan.
New York Museum of Ice Cream May of this year (2021) I traveled to New York City and visited the museum of ice cream in Manhattan New York. My family and I got to take pictures with the various different set ups they had representing ice cream cake and other desserts.
Tallahassee Sakura Whenever I’m looking for a nice feeling quick meal I like to visit Sakura on Monroe here in Tallahassee, Florida. You can never go wrong with sweeet potato rolls, cucumber rolls, or the classic avocado rolls. Other times when I’m feeling for something fried, I’ll get the vegetable tempura with some rice.
Atlanta Ponce City Market My dad lives in Atlanta, Georgia. When I get the opportunity to visit him I like to visit Ponce City Market where you can always find something really good to eat. Ponce city market is a really nice place to just go and hang out and in the winter they even have ice-skating on the rooftop.
